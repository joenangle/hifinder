# Standalone DAC Pricing Research (2025/2026)

**Research Date:** February 20, 2026
**Total DACs Researched:** 42 models
**ASR Coverage:** 20/42 models (48%) with SINAD measurements

---

## Executive Summary

This document contains comprehensive pricing research for popular standalone DACs (Digital-to-Analog Converters) in the audiophile headphone community. All prices are **current retail prices for NEW units** as of 2025/2026, sourced from:

- Manufacturer websites (Schiit.com, Topping official, etc.)
- Authorized dealers (Audio46, Drop, Apos Audio, Headphones.com)
- Audiophile review sites (Audio Science Review, Head-Fi)

**IMPORTANT:** These are STANDALONE DACs only. DAC/amp combo units are excluded.

## Data Quality Notes

### Sound Signature Accuracy:
- **Neutral:** Most delta-sigma DACs (Topping, SMSL, Benchmark)
- **Warm:** Most R2R DACs (Denafrips, Holo Audio, Chord, Audio-GD, Schiit Bifrost)
- **Bright:** Rare in DACs (none identified in this research)

### ASR SINAD Measurement Coverage:
- **Budget tier:** 7/9 models (78%) - Good coverage
- **Mid tier:** 8/12 models (67%) - Decent coverage
- **High tier:** 4/12 models (33%) - Poor coverage (many R2R not measured)
- **Summit tier:** 1/9 models (11%) - Very poor (exotic DACs rarely measured)

**Note:** R2R DACs generally don't measure as well on SINAD tests but are valued for subjective sound quality.
