# Quick Amplifier Reference - Most Recommended Models by Tier

## Budget Tier ($100-$200) - "Best Bang for Buck"

### Top 5 Most Recommended:

1. **Schiit Magni+/Heresy** - $109
   - Clean, powerful, great measurements
   - Heresy = ruler-flat, Magni+ = slightly warmer
   - Ideal for: HD6XX, DT 770/990, most budget planars

2. **JDS Labs Atom Amp+** - $99 (currently in DB)
   - ASR favorite, 115 dB SINAD
   - Ultra-clean, high power for the price
   - Ideal for: Entry-level planar, Sennheiser HD series

3. **Topping L30 II** - $160 (currently in DB)
   - 121 dB SINAD, 3.5W @ 32Ω
   - Extremely clean measurements
   - Ideal for: Measurement-focused users

4. **Schiit Magnius** - $199
   - Balanced XLR in/out
   - 5W @ 32Ω balanced
   - Ideal for: Users wanting balanced on budget

   - Ideal for: DT 990, T1, Beyer treble

## Matching Amps to Headphones

### For High Impedance (>250Ω):
- Schiit Valhalla 2 (tube)
- Schiit Asgard 3
- Rupert Neve RNHP
- Any high power amp (HPA4, V280, etc.)

### For Low Impedance Sensitive (<32Ω):
- Topping L30 II (ultra-low noise floor)
- JDS Labs Atom Amp+
- Benchmark HPA4 (perfect)

### For Planar (needs power):
- Singxer SA-1 (budget power)
- Flux Lab Volot (mid-tier power)
- Ferrum OOR (high-tier power)
- Violectric V550 (summit power)
- HeadAmp GS-X mk2 (ultimate power)

### For Bright Headphones (DT 990, T1, etc.):
- Schiit Asgard 3 (warm Class A)
- Geshelli Archel 3 (warm-ish)
- Burson Soloist 3X (discrete warm)
- Tube amps (Valhalla 2, WA6-SE)

- Neutral amps: Magni Heresy, Atom Amp+
