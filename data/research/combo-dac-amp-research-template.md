## RESEARCH CHECKLIST

### For Each Device, Verify:
- [ ] **Availability:** Currently sold new (not discontinued)
- [ ] **Category:** Confirm it's a COMBO (has both DAC and amp)
- [ ] **Pricing:** Check manufacturer + 2-3 retailers for accurate price
- [ ] **Specs:** Power output (critical for recommendations)
- [ ] **ASR Review:** Search ASR forum for measurements
- [ ] **Sound Signature:** Based on reviews/measurements
- [ ] **Form Factor:** Desktop vs portable vs dongle

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid:
1. **DAPs (Digital Audio Players):** These are music players with storage, not simple DAC/amps
2. **Stacks:** Separate DAC + amp units that stack (Schiit Modi+Magni, SMSL SU-6+SH-6)
3. **Discontinued Models:** BTR3, E10K, older generations
4. **Amp-Only:** Schiit Magni, Atom Amp (no DAC)
5. **DAC-Only:** Modi, SMSL Sanskrit (no amp)
